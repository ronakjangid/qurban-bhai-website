We create that Experiences

Butter Concepts is a young multi-disciplinary design studio based out of Ahmedabad who delves into Architecture, Interior Design and Visual Identity. We work on various scales starting from Architecture of residential / commercial spaces to their interiors with bespoke furniture designing. In case of commercial projects we create and weave the overall visual identity of the company in its spaces. Our design philosophy is to explore materials, palettes, forms and details while developing meaningful and efficient spaces / products.

Dharal Surelia is the Founder and Principal Architect at Butter Concepts. An architect, designer and an artist based in India. Passionate about all forms of Art & Design be it spaces, products, philosophies or experiences. A fresh Graduate from Babson College with a Masters of Science in Entrepreneurial Leadership and CEPT University with a Bachelors in Architecture. In the pursuit of fusing the domains of design and entrepreneurship.
