Blooming Wellness

Aspiring to create a quaint getaway from the usual hustle, the client approached our team with the brief to design his second home. Envisioned as a place of respite and meditation, The Blooming Wellness.

Completion Year: 2022
Gross Built Area (ft2): 2100 sqft
Project Location: Ahmedabad
Program: Residential

The project began with an inquiry in Blue, the signature colour that the client  identifies with. We wanted to create a space which experiments with the colour in many forms and finishes. Ranging from the veneer finishes to accent walls, all elements within the apartment symbiotically come together; never letting the colour over power or hinder the visual calmness. All shades of Blue are then balanced with Greys, with a pop of terracotta for contrast.

Real comfort,
visual and physical,
is vital to every room.
